Pepperoni Stromboli

Image

Description

OK so here's how 'ya get a real good ass Stromboli. Made using only the best itiaian deli meats and mozzarealla that comes grated in a neon bag. Welcome to flavor town.

Ingredients

  • Pepperoni
  • Salami
  • Capacola
  • Other Salami
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pizza Dough... the good shit
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Semolina dough
  2. Flatten dough on board
  3. Layer Meats
  4. add cheese
  5. gloss with garlic olive oil
  6. Begin to roll long way ,creating a pin wheel
  7. Tuck and seal final edge
  8. Glaze with garlic olive oil
  9. Insert in 450 degree Oven for 20 mins.
  10. take out and let rest
  11. slice short way to create pin wheels
  12. Enjoy!
